About

Welcome to the CyberSoup Blog!

We’re a passionate couple driven by a shared fascination with cybersecurity, digital privacy and secure coding practices. Together, we explore, research and share insights into the world of cybersecurity to help make the digital landscape safer for everyone.

Our journey began with a curiosity about how systems work and deal with modern cybersecurity challenges. The passion for building the right solutions in the right way has gradually evolved into a deep commitment to studying and educating ourselves and others on topics like identity and access management, DevSecOps and application security. With backgrounds in cybersecurity research and teaching, and having gained hands-on experience in the industry, we’re dedicated to making complex security concepts approachable and actionable for everyone.

CyberSoup is your one-stop shop for technical articles, security best practices, research findings and real-world case studies. Our goal is to empower readers at all levels — whether you’re an industry professional, student, or enthusiast — to build a solid understanding of cybersecurity.

Join us as we dive into the latest in cyber defense, share educational resources, and contribute to a more secure and privacy-conscious world.
